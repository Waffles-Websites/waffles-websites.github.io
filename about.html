About Cedar Grove Christian Church

We are a community of believers committed to knowing Christ, growing in His Word, and sharing His love with our neighbors.

Our Story

Cedar Grove Christian Church has been serving the Chuckey, Tennessee community for decades. Our heart is to be a welcoming place where people from all walks of life can encounter the life-changing message of the Gospel.

Whether you are new to faith, returning after time away, or looking for a church family, you’ll find open arms, Biblical teaching, and a commitment to walking together in Christ.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to glorify God by making disciples who love God, love others, and live out the Gospel. We gather each week to worship, study God’s Word, and encourage one another to live faithfully wherever God has placed us.
