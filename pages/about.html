Our Story

Cedar Grove Christian Church has been a place of faith, family, and fellowship in Chuckey, TN for generations. We're a Bible-believing church passionate about loving God and loving others.

We believe in cultivating a Christ-centered community where everyone feels welcomed and encouraged to grow in their faith.
